Switching transactional email providers by flipping all traffic at once on a Friday is a recipe for deliverability disasters. The article recommends applying canary deployment principles to email provider migrations, gradually routing increasing percentages through the new provider while monitoring deliverability metrics against the baseline. AhaSend is positioned as the destination provider for this safer, phased migration approach.
