GDPR applies to any product sending transactional emails to EU users, covering not just marketing but also password resets, order confirmations, and account notifications. Developers often underestimate GDPR's scope and lack practical knowledge about transactional email compliance obligations and proper email provider selection. The guide walks through the requirements and best practices for GDPR-compliant transactional email delivery.
